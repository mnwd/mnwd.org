Here at MNWD we want to:

Provide a creative workspace for web projects.

Many university students do not have enough opportunity to be creative. At Minnesota Web Development, we believe that creativity is extremely important and we strive to provide a space to foster such creativity.

Promote critical advice and group collaboration.

Critical feedback is crucial for projects and can lead to significantly better results. We want to connect students with the common interest of web development to encourage collaboration and constructive criticism.

Strengthen Web Community Relationships.

There are many opportunities in the professional world both around campus and in the greater Twin Cities area for Web Developers and Designers to connect. We want to help students who find these connections and build quality relationships in the community.

Bridge the gap between web developers and web designers.

A longstanding issue in web development is mismatched expectations: developers want a clean technical solution and designers want high aesthetic quality. Our goal is to increase communication and mutual understanding between designers and developers.

Create a knowledge base and support system for web-development topics.

Learning web development is frustrating and sorting through the abundant resources is time consuming. We aim to build a knowledge base of hand-picked resources by our community.
