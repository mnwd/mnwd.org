Welcome!

MNWD is a UofM Twin Cities Student Group for everything web! We hold regular meetings, workshops, and fun off-campus activities. Feel free to stop by a meeting to check us out anytime!

We will continue to update this website with resources and news, but we know it's not very convenient for you to have to keep coming back. Luckily, we have a Facebook page! Feel free to like us here.

— Ben, Brian, and Dan

Why so plain?

You might be wondering why the site looks so plain. First, we have not developed our brand, as we want it to be a group effort. Second, we want the site to be available as a playground for members. And, finally, we wanted something very soon that would be easy to maintain. The obvious choice to accomplish this was Bootstrap. Check it out if you haven't!

The short answer is that more is coming soon!
